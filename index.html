Michael Joseph Jackson

The King of Pop

In the photo, Michael Jackson, who is considered by many the greatest artist of all time.

Here's a time line of Jackson's life:

  • 1958 - Born in Gary, Indiana
  • 1964 - Joined their brothers' band, the Jackson 5
  • 1979 - Recorded his first solo album, Off the Wall.
  • 1983 - Released his most successful album, Thriller
  • 1988 - Acquired and moved to the 'Neverland' ranch
  • 1989 - Became known as the 'King of Pop'
  • 2001 - Released his final album, Invincible
  • 2009 - Died by the age of 50
